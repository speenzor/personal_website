My Past

Worked six jobs spanning politics, janitorial services, real estate, finance, Uber driver, retail, and freelance. Since graduating from Whitman College in 2014 with a degree in economics I have worked at several diverse jobs. I started out working for a real estate development firm called StoneCreek Company doing due diligence work for several projects, including the acquisition of a retail property that was successfully repositioned to retail and medical office. After I gained valuable knowledge of the real estate industry and developed great attention to detail I desired to move into the financial side of real estate and obtained a position at Lucent Capital, a highly respected commercial real estate mortgage broker in Beverly Hills. In 2016, while working at Lucent Capital, I personally paid for and completed the Investment Banking institute course with investment banker and friend, Chuck Bush, of Great Road Capital. This course takes place over four weekends and it is a significant financial and time commitment that honed my skills in financial modeling, including LBO and merger models.
